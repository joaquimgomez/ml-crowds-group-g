AttributesAirTransmissionModelDroplets

Attributes related to droplets that are shared among all droplets. These are not defined for each instance of Droplets separately to keep the class lean.

Field: emissionFrequency

Describes how often Droplets are emitted by an infectious pedestrian. Unit: 1 / second

Field: distanceOfSpread

Describes the shape of AttributesDroplets: Radius of the circular segment. Unit: meter

Field: lifeTime

Describes the persistence of Droplets. Unit: second

Field: pathogenLoad

Describes the pathogen load within Droplets. It remains constant over lifeTime. Unit: particles

Field: angleOfSpreadInDeg

Describes the shape of AttributesDroplets: Angle of the circular segment. Unit: degree

Field: absorptionRate

Describes the fraction infectious particles inhaled from droplets if the inhaling agent is located within droplets. Unit: 1 / inhalation
