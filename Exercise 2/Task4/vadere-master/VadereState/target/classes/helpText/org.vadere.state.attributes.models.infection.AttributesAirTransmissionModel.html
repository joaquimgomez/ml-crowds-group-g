AttributesAirTransmissionModel

AttributesAirTransmissionModel contains user-defined properties related to the AirTransmissionModel. They define properties that are shared by all instances of

  • Pedestrian
  • AerosolCloud
  • Droplets

Field: dropletsActive

Defines whether droplets are considered in the exposure model (true) or not (false).

Field: aerosolCloudsActive

Defines whether aerosol clouds are considered in the exposure model (true) or not (false).
